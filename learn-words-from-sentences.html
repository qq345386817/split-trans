Guide

How to learn words from sentences

Use PhraseCut as a sentence workbench while reading real content.

Collect one useful sentence

Choose a sentence from something you actually read: an article, email, subtitle, song lyric, message, or book.

Understand before saving

Read the full translation first, then inspect the words and phrases that carry the meaning.

Keep the reusable parts

Save the sentence, the important phrase, or the individual word depending on what you expect to use again.

Use PhraseCut for this workflow

Download PhraseCut on iPhone, iPad, and Mac to turn real sentences into saved study material.
