Guide

Export language study materials

Your study library should not be trapped in one app.

What gets exported

PhraseCut exports item type, language pair, title, meaning, source sentence, sentence translation, notes, review metadata, and timestamps.

When CSV is enough

Use CSV when you want a spreadsheet, backup, or general-purpose study archive.

When to use Anki-compatible CSV

Use the Anki-compatible export when you want to continue reviewing saved material in an external spaced-repetition workflow.

Use PhraseCut for this workflow

Download PhraseCut on iPhone, iPad, and Mac to turn real sentences into saved study material.
