Common questions

Is PhraseCut only a translator?

No. Translation is part of the workflow, but PhraseCut is organized around learning: context, saved study items, review, and export.

What can I save?

You can save words, phrases, and full sentences with the sentence context and translation.

Can I export my study materials?

Yes. The Library can export CSV or Anki-compatible CSV files for your own workflow.
