How it works

From difficult sentence to reusable study asset

  1. Paste a sentence from an article, email, subtitle, or website.
  2. Read the natural translation and inspect word or phrase meaning.
  3. Save the parts you want to remember into your learning library.
  4. Review saved items later and export them to CSV or Anki-compatible files.

FAQ

Common questions

Is Sentwise only a sentence translator?

No. It is designed for language learning. The goal is not only to translate a sentence, but to help you understand and keep useful parts for later review.

What can I save in the library?

You can save words, phrases, and full sentences, then review them later or export them to CSV or Anki-compatible files.

Where can I find support and privacy information?

Visit the Help page for usage guidance and the Privacy Policy for data handling details.
