PhraseCut Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 6, 2026

Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how PhraseCut and the companion website at luopeike.com/split-trans handle information.

App data

PhraseCut is designed to keep your study data on your device. Your saved items, review data, and app settings are stored locally unless you export them yourself.

Personal information

We do not require you to create an account to use the app. We do not directly collect your name, address, or similar personal profile information through the app.

Analytics and tracking

We do not use third-party analytics or advertising SDKs on this website, and we do not use the app to build advertising profiles about you.

Exports and user actions

If you choose to export your library to CSV or Anki-compatible files, that export is initiated by you. The resulting file is handled through the sharing or storage destination you choose.

Website

This website is used to provide product information, help pages, and the privacy policy. If you email support, your email provider will process the message according to its own privacy practices.

Children's privacy

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children through the app or this website.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will publish the updated version on this page with a revised effective date.

Contact

If you have privacy questions, contact cameoshell09@gmail.com.
