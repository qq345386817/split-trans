How to use Sentwise

This app is built for sentence-based language learning. Use the sections below to understand the main workflow: analyze a sentence, save useful items, review them later, and export your study materials.

1. Learn from a sentence

  1. Open the Learn tab.
  2. Paste or type a sentence you want to understand.
  3. Choose the source and target language if needed.
  4. Run the analysis to view the full translation and word or phrase meaning.

The Learn screen focuses on one sentence at a time: first understand the full meaning, then inspect the important words or phrases, and finally save what is worth reviewing.

What the Learn screen is for

Sentwise is designed to help you learn through real sentences instead of isolated word lists. The app highlights the sentence meaning first, then shows the useful parts you may want to keep for later review.

2. Save useful words, phrases, or sentences

When you find something worth keeping, save it to your study library. The app can store not only the item itself, but also the sentence it came from and its meaning in context.

3. Review saved items

Open the Review tab to revisit the items you saved. This helps you return to useful vocabulary before you forget it.

4. Manage your study library

In the Library tab you can browse the words, phrases, and sentences you collected from real reading and study sessions.

5. Export your study materials

Use export options in the app to send your library to CSV or Anki-compatible files, so your study materials remain portable.
