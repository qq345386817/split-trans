Guide

Sentence-based vocabulary learning

Vocabulary sticks better when the word stays connected to the sentence where you found it.

Why context matters

A word list can tell you a definition, but a sentence shows tone, grammar, collocation, and the situation where the word is useful.

What to save

Save the full sentence when it contains a pattern you want to reuse. Save a word or phrase when it is useful on its own but still needs context.

Review with meaning attached

When you review later, the original sentence helps you remember why the item mattered in the first place.

Use PhraseCut for this workflow
